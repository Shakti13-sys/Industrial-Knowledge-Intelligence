INCIDENT REPORT: INC-2022-014
Facility: Vizag Chemical Processing Plant
Date of incident: 2022-09-08
Classification: Near miss — mechanical failure, no injury, no release

SUMMARY
PUMP-204 (Unit 4 feed line, centrifugal transfer pump, CP-200 series)
experienced a bearing seizure during routine operation. Operators noted
unusual noise approximately 40 minutes before automatic trip on high
vibration. No product release occurred. Unit 4 was down for 8 hours during
repair.

ROOT CAUSE
Investigation found the bearing had not been greased in approximately 20
months, exceeding the OEM-recommended 12-month grease interval (see OEM
Manual, Section 3.3). Contributing factor: the plant's CMMS had this asset
scheduled on a 24-month bearing inspection cycle, inconsistent with OEM
guidance of 18 months.

CORRECTIVE ACTIONS
1. Bearing replaced and grease schedule reset (completed 2022-09-09).
2. CMMS inspection interval flagged for correction from 24 to 18 months —
   assigned to Reliability Engineering, target date 2022-10-15.
3. Recommend vibration monitoring be added to this asset's routine rounds.

REGULATORY NOTE
This incident was reviewed against OISD-STD-118 provisions for hydrocarbon
service line equipment; no violation was found, as no release occurred, but
the near-miss was logged per internal safety reporting policy.

STATUS: Corrective action #2 not yet confirmed complete as of report date.
