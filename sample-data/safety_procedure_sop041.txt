SAFETY PROCEDURE SOP-SF-041
Title: Rotating Equipment Vibration Monitoring — Unit 4 Feed Line
Facility: Vizag Chemical Processing Plant
Revision: 3 (supersedes Rev 2, dated 2021-05-01)

1. PURPOSE
Establish routine vibration monitoring for rotating equipment in hydrocarbon
service to detect bearing degradation before failure, in line with
OISD-STD-118 inspection expectations for fire protection-critical rotating
equipment.

2. SCOPE
Applies to all CP-200 series pumps in Unit 4, including PUMP-204, PUMP-205,
and PUMP-206.

3. PROCEDURE
3.1 Weekly handheld vibration readings shall be taken at the drive-end and
non-drive-end bearing housings.
3.2 Readings above 3.5 mm/s RMS shall be logged and escalated to the
Reliability Engineer within 24 hours.
3.3 Readings above 4.5 mm/s RMS require immediate equipment isolation review.

4. REVISION HISTORY
Rev 3 (this revision): Added explicit escalation timeline after Reliability
Review Board findings related to INC-2022-014 identified inconsistent
escalation practices across shifts.
Rev 2: Added CP-200 series scope.
Rev 1: Initial issue.

5. TRAINING REQUIREMENT
All Unit 4 operations personnel must complete vibration monitoring training
before being authorized to record readings independently.
